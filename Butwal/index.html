About Butwal Transportation System

Butwal Sub-Metropolitan City has developed an organized transportation network with multiple stations serving different routes. The official document includes:

  • 15+ major bus stations with route information
  • Official taxi stands with designated zones
  • Microbus terminals for inter-city travel
  • Contact information for station managers
