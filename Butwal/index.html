About These Images

These 10 images represent the complete set of training results for Butwal Sub-Metropolitan City Transportation Department Training 2025. The images include certificates for successful participants, pass lists with scores, station assignments for job placements, and detailed score sheets for both theory and practical examinations.

Contact for verification: Training Department, Butwal Municipal Office - 071-540789 or training@butwalmun.gov.np
